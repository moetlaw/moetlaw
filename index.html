Photo URLs (Shared via Google Sheets)
Add photo URLs directly in your Google Sheet (column B) next to the key. Photos update automatically on the next sync and are visible to all viewers.

How to get the URL from Google Drive:
1. Upload photo to Google Drive → right-click → Share → Anyone with the link
2. Copy the link — it looks like: https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view
3. Change it to: https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=FILE_ID

emp_photo_url — Employee of the Month
ph1_url through ph10_url — Photo Slides 1–10
